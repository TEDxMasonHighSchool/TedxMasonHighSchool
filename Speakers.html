Geoff Thatcher
Geoff’s eclectic background always delivers a fresh perspective as his experience includes everything from a traveling exhibition in Saudi Arabia to the American Airlines Museum in Texas. Whether it’s the world’s largest video dome on a beach in Dubai or the opening ceremonies of the Rio 2007 Pan American Games in Maracanā Stadium, Geoff has been there. Recent and current projects include branding a new indoor theme park, creating a new special effects theater and brand experience center for FM Global in Singapore, leading the creative for the grand opening of Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi and publishing a book on growing up in a theme park.
Dr. Christopher Lewis
Christopher Lewis, MD is a native of Cincinnati whose work revolves around service to others. He is a family physician for UC Health and Associate Professor of Family Medicine at the University of Cincinnati College of Medicine. He also strives to make an impact in education, serving as the Vice Provost for Academic Programs at UC. He received his bachelor’s degree in Biology at Harvard University, then attended the University of Cincinnati College of Medicine for medical school and residency. Dr. Lewis founded the Village Life Outreach Project, a non-profit organization that partners with communities in Tanzania, East Africa to “unite communities to promote Life, Health, and Education.” Locally, he is a member of the Cincinnati Board of Health and the Cincinnati Medical Association. Dr. Lewis is passionate about his family, including his wife Alicia and daughters Grace and Vivian.
